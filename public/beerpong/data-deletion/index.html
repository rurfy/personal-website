Data Deletion

Beerpong Companion — Account & data removal instructions

You can permanently delete your Beerpong Companion account and all associated personal data directly from within the app. No email or web form required.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Beerpong Companion app
  2. Tap the Profile tab (bottom navigation)
  3. Tap your profile icon or name to open Account settings
  4. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your user profile (username, display name, email)
  • Your friends list
  • Your in-app purchases and cosmetic items
  • Your Firebase Authentication account
Note: Game records you participated in may be retained in anonymised form to preserve the integrity of other players’ match history. Your personal identity is removed from these records.

Alternative: Contact Us

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion by email, contact us at chrissy.richter2710@gmail.com with your registered email address. We will process your request within 30 days.
