Privacy Policy

Beerpong Companion — Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Christopher Richter (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Beerpong Companion mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in, we collect the following information via Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In:

  • Your display name
  • Your email address
  • A unique user identifier from Firebase Authentication

During normal app usage, we also store:

  • Your chosen in-app username
  • Game statistics (cups made, cups missed, wins, losses)
  • Friends list (other users you have added)
  • In-app purchases and cosmetic items you have unlocked

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To identify your account and display your profile
  • To track and display your game statistics
  • To enable the friends and multiplayer features
  • To restore your purchases and progress across devices

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) — handles secure sign-in
  • Cloud Firestore (Google LLC) — stores your profile and game data
  • Google Sign-In (Google LLC) — authenticates your Google account
  • Sign in with Apple (Apple Inc.) — authenticates your Apple account

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. Game records you participated in may be retained in anonymised form after account deletion to preserve the integrity of other players’ statistics.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data

To delete your account, use the Delete Account option in the app under Profile → Delete Account. Alternatively, contact us at chrissy.richter2710@gmail.com.

6. Security

Your data is stored securely on Google’s Firebase infrastructure. We do not store passwords; authentication is handled entirely by Google and Apple.

7. Children

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact: chrissy.richter2710@gmail.com
